## Books

- Can't Hurt Me — Master Your Mind and Defy the Odds (David Goggins, 2018)
  notes: Primary spine. 107K words. Each chapter ends with a Challenge — the named Goggins frameworks (Accountability Mirror, Cookie Jar, Taking Souls, 40% Rule, Visualization, Callused Mind, Hard Path) live in these challenges.

- Living with a SEAL (Jesse Itzler, 2015)
  notes: THIRD-PARTY BIOGRAPHICAL. Itzler narrates 30 days of Goggins living in his house. 55K words, mostly Itzler's voice. Mine for verbatim Goggins quotes (formatted as dialogue) only. Do NOT extract voice samples or frameworks unless cross-confirmed in Goggins's own books.

- Never Finished — Unshackle Your Mind and Win the War Within (David Goggins, 2022)
  notes: Secondary spine. 84K words. Sequel; introduces the "unshackled mind" frame, the "Mental Lab", and deeper material on "the savage", "the empowerment of failure". Note — "Who's gonna carry the boats?" slogan does NOT appear verbatim in the books; it's sourced from on-stage talks (see nxyO1Pv8CbU).

- Can't Hurt Me Audiobook — Goggins narration + Goggins/Skolnick bonus commentary
  notes: 150K words (43K more than epub). The delta is the bonus tracks where Goggins and Adam Skolnick discuss each chapter podcast-style — unique source not in the epub. Audio held outside repo.
