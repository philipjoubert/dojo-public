# Jensen Huang — Source Manifest

Corpus for Jensen Huang (co-founder and CEO of Nvidia). Voice-safety tagging is per `DOJO-PERSONA-PROCESS.md`:
- `type: solo` — Jensen alone (keynotes, commencement speeches). Safe for voice samples.
- `type: interview-guest:HOST` — Jensen is THE interviewee on someone else's show / panel. Safe for voice samples; host's framing is bracketed in the transcript.
- `type: dual-fireside:COUNTERPART` — Jensen on-stage with another equally-famous figure (Zuckerberg, Mensch). Both voices are prominent and auto-captions aren't diarized. **Do not quote directly** — context only. Both participants' lines get mixed in the transcript.
- `type: third-party` — Someone else's analysis ABOUT Jensen (Founders Podcast, video essays). **Never use for voice samples.** Useful only for framework identification, and even then the quotes are derivative (host reading from Kim's book etc).
- Books authored by others are third-party biographies — quote-extraction only, never voice samples.
- **NVIDIA shareholder letters (2011–2025) are primary-source Jensen-written prose** — the only written-declarative voice register in the corpus. Use heavily for manifesto / written voice samples. The markdown files are the first 10 pages of each annual report; the "Dear Stakeholders" letter portion is embedded inside, typically 1–3K words, surrounded by cover-page and TOC noise. Extract just the letter portion before voice-sampling.
