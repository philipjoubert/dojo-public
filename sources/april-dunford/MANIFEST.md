Authoritative inventory of processed source material. YouTube entries carry a `type:` tag per DOJO-PERSONA-PROCESS.md Core Principle 5 (voice contamination). Tags:

- `type: solo` — April alone. Safe for voice samples and direct quotation.
- `type: interview-host:GUEST` — April hosting *The Positioning Show* with a named guest. **Mixed voice. Context only — never quote; never extract a framework whose only source is an episode of this type.**
- `type: interview-guest:HOST` — April as THE interviewee on someone else's show. Safe for voice (she's doing most of the talking).
- Books and articles authored by April are implicitly `type: author`.
