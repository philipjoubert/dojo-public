# Clayton Christensen — source manifest

**Domain:** Disruptive innovation, Jobs-to-Be-Done, theory-building strategy, "measuring your life."

**Voice-provenance summary:** 6 books (authored) + 13 full HBR articles (authored) + 1 MIT Sloan article + 10 Christensen Institute theory/research pages + 2 interview articles + 5 YouTube transcripts (3 solo, 2 interview-guest — all safe for quotation; no interview-host episodes in the corpus).

**Corpus total:** ~710K words. Primary voice spine is the books; HBR articles give the tight declarative register; YouTube transcripts (especially the Oxford Clarendon Lecture and JTBD monologues) carry the conversational teaching voice.

**Corpus note:** The file originally labeled `innovators-solution.epub` turned out to be *The Innovator's Prescription* (2009, healthcare); renamed and kept. The actual 2003 *Innovator's Solution* (Christensen + Raynor) was added separately from PDF as `innovators-solution-2003.pdf` / `innovators-solution.md`. All four Christensen core books now present.
