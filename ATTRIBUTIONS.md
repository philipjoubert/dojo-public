## A note on voice samples

Each persona contains 3 voice samples of 300–500 words each — verbatim excerpts from the expert's own writing or speech. These are used for the skill construction purpose only: to teach Claude the rhythm and word choice of each expert. They are not the work being sold; the skill construction is the work.

If you are a rights holder or representative and want a sample swapped, refined, or removed, open an issue on this repository.
