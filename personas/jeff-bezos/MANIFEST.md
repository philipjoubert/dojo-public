## Books

- Amazon Unbound — Jeff Bezos and the Invention of a Global Empire (Brad Stone, 2021)
  notes: Sequel to The Everything Store. Same rule — voice from direct quotes only.

- Invent and Wander — The Collected Writings of Jeff Bezos (Jeff Bezos, 2021)
  notes: Primary Bezos voice source. Collects 1997–2020 shareholder letters plus speeches.

- The Bezos Blueprint (Carmine Gallo, 2022)
  notes: Commentary on Bezos's writing style and communication mechanisms. Context source.

- The Everything Store — Jeff Bezos and the Age of Amazon (Brad Stone, 2013)
  notes: Biographer prose — use for factual grounding; pull voice only from direct Bezos quotes.

- Working Backwards — Insights, Stories, and Secrets from Inside Amazon (Colin Bryar & Bill Carr, 2021)
  notes: Insider account of Amazon mechanisms (PR-FAQ, six-pager, two-pizza teams). Primary source for mechanism-based frameworks.
